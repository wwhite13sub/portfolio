About me

I AM SO EXCITED ABOUT MY CAREER AS A WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPER!!!!!!!

I did well in high school but had to work full time my junior and senior years. After graduating high school, I worked at an electronics store, shoe store, insurance company and a shipping company. I did well at these positions but I was just “treading water”.

I decided one day that I needed to MAKE A CHANGE and start a career doing something that I really enjoy and that provides a decent salary. I did extremely well in my college computer science class and started investigating web application development. I enrolled in TEXAS A&M Full Stack Web Development and have created multiple web applications using a variety of technologies.

IT’S MY THING ---- it’s like solving a puzzle (and my analytical mind likes that) by making the software do exactly what I need for it to do to create the best user experiences.
