About Me

I AM SO EXCITED ABOUT MY CAREER AS A WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPER!!!!!

I made A/B's as a high school junior and senior even though I worked full time. After high school graduation, I always worked two jobs and received good reviews from my managers- but I was just treading water.

I decided to pursue a career that I would enjoy and where I could earn a decent salary. I made an A in my college computer science course so I investigated Web Application development and completed the Texas A&M Full Stack Web Application course. I currently have two websites live in production: Yarbrough Real Estate Leasing and my personal website. I completed and implemented four other class web applications that utilize a variety of technologies.

WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT IS MY THING!! ---- I get to use my design and technical skills, create the best user experiences and am continuously learning .
